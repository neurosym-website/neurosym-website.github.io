Overview

In almost every field of science, it is now possible to capture and process large amounts of data, but the process of drawing insights and theories from that data remains a largely manual process that requires scientists to generate hypotheses consistent with the data and to devise new experiments that can be used to test those hypotheses.

The goal of our project is to develop novel neurosymbolic techniques that incorporate prior knowledge about a process and can produce interpretable models that can be analyzed to devise new experiments or to infer causal relations between different parts of a process.
