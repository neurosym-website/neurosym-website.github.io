Summer school on Neurosymbolic Programming July 11-13 2022

Sponsorship opportunities We are seeking sponsors to help make the The summer school a success and to help ensure that a broad cross section of students can attend and benefit from the experience regardless of their funding situation. Our target for the summer school is to have 80 participants, although as of April 30, we already had 117 applicants with 73% being graduate students and 11.4% being undergraduates, the rest being academic and industrial researchers. 42% of our applicants so far have expressed that they would only be able to attend if they receive some financial aid.

Specifically, we are asking companies to sponsor travel grants for students. The cost of sponsoring each student would be $960. For each student that the company sponsors, the student will receive a travel grant that will reimburse them for their airfare and hotel for the three days of the event. The grant rate per student was calculated based on our quoted hotel costs (assuming 2 students per room) and on the airfare rates from different cities to LA as of the end of April. Any funds left over after funding the travel grants will be used to help cover some of the costs associated with running the event (mainly food).

Every student funded by a travel grant from the company will receive an e-mail that will include an acknowledgement that the sponsoring company will be able to customize. Sponsoring companies will be included in the event website and will be acknowledged during the opening presentation every day of the summer school. Additional benefits will depend on the number of students sponsored by a company as detailed below.

# of students Benefits
1 to 5 students Companies sponsoring 1 to 5 students will be acknowledged as Bronze Sponsors in both the website and the opening ceremony.
6 to 20 Companies sponsoring 6 to 15 students will be acknowledged as Silver Sponsors in both the website and the opening ceremony. One company representative will be invited to attend the summer school and they will be introduced as sponsors during the opening day presentation and will have the opportunity to network with event participants.
20 and above Companies sponsoring over 20 students will be acknowledged as Gold Sponsors in both the website and the opening ceremony. Up to 3 company representatives will be invited to attend the summer school and they will be allowed to set up a table at the event to promote their company to participants.
